Meet Dr. Zuleikha Tyebjee

My name is Zuleikha Tyebjee. I am the founder of Mindful Medical Weight Loss and am board certified in Family Medicine and board eligible in Obesity Medicine. I developed my weight loss program while working as a primary care physician and quickly came to realize the need for a sustainable and approachable path to weight loss.

At MMWL, we provide lifelong weight loss through:

Dietary programs tailored to each patient
